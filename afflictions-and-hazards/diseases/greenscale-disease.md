# Greenscale

**Source** [_Heart of the Jungle pg. 7_](http://paizo.com/store/downloads/pathfinder/pathfinderChronicles/pathfinderRPG/v5748btpy8evh)  
**Type** injury; **Fortitude Save** DC 15  
**Onset** 1d6 days; **Frequency** 1/week
