# Hunt of the Ankou

**Source** [_Horror Adventures pg. 138_](http://paizo.com/products/btpy9n5a?Pathfinder-Roleplaying-Game-Horror-Adventures)

**Effect** The victim is hunted by an [[ankou|ankou]] as described in [[hunt-of-the-bogeyman-curse|hunt of the bogeyman]].  
**Cure** Only a [[miracle|miracle]] or [[wish|wish]] can free the victim.
